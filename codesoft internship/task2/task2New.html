ABOUT US


This is a advertising page for Netflix Netflix is a streaming service that provides a wide
variety of TV shows, movies, documentaries, and more on
thousands of internet-connected devices. Subscribers can
watch as much as they want, anytime, anywhere, without any
commercials, all for a monthly fee. Netflix also produces
its own original content, known as Netflix Originals, which
includes popular series like "Stranger Things," "The Crown,"
"The Witcher." The service started as a DVD rental-by-mail
business in 1997 before expanding into streaming in 2007.

MOVIES

On Netflix, you can find a variety of popular movies such as
"The Irishman" (2019), a crime drama directed by Martin Scorsese
and starring Robert De Niro, Al Pacino, and Joe Pesci, which
follows World War II veteran Frank Sheeran as he reflects on
his life as a hitman for the Bufalino crime family and his
involvement in the disappearance of labor leader Jimmy Hoffa.
Another notable film is "Marriage Story" (2019), directed by
Noah Baumbach and featuring Scarlett Johansson and Adam Driver,
which explores the emotional complexities of a couple navigating
a coast-to-coast divorce while trying to maintain their family.
For action enthusiasts, "Extraction" (2020), starring Chris
Hemsworth, is a thrilling film about a black-market mercenary
on a mission to rescue the kidnapped son of an international
crime lord, facing numerous obstacles in a dangerous underworld.
